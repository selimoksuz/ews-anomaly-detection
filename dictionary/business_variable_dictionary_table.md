# Business Variable Dictionary Table

Bu tablo, Ticari Orta segmenti icin business degiskenlerinin implementation-grade ozet kontratidir.

## Column Notes

- `Portfolio Scope`
  - `general`: Ticari Orta icinde genis kapsama sahip aile
  - `conditional_general`: genel kullanima aday ama applicability/coverage guard gerekir
  - `segment_specific`: yalniz belirli alt portfoylerde anlamli
  - `not_model_scope`: feature degil, rule/filter
- `Model Placement`
  - `broad_core`: genel modelin omurgasina dogrudan girer
  - `conditional_core`: ayni genel modele guardrail ile girer
  - `segment_specific`: yalniz ilgili alt portfoy/submodel icinde modele girer
  - `enrichment_only`: modele girmez, sadece baglam/aciklama icindir
  - `rule_only`: deterministic rule/filter katmanina gider
- `Population Reference Recommended`
  - `yes`: acik peer/population feature uretilir
  - `no`: uretilmez; mevcut anomaly motoru population pattern'i zaten ogrenir
- `Annualization`
  - `Q1 * 4`, `Q2 * 2`, `Q3 * 4/3`, `YE * 1`
- `FS Freshness`
  - business sert kural: son 12 ayda mali veri yoksa kayit uretilmez / feature yok sayilir
